ABOUT

Culminating from the duo-inspired ethos of “minimum members, maximum sound,”
Los Angeles’ The Haunts is a band born out of the goal of stripping down garage rock
from its simple beginnings to an even rawer, reverb-soaked sound. With the trifecta of
drummer Alanna Swadlow’s precise, electric style, guitarist Aaron Brotman’s punk
meets surf fusion, and the final component of vocalist Max Collier’s smooth croon,
The Haunts continue to spread their brand of surfy garage rock to the West Coast and beyond.
